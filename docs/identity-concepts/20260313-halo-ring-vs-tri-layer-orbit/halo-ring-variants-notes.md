# HALOS Halo Ring Refinement Notes

These variants refine the Halo Ring direction after initial concept review.

## Variants

- `halo-ring-variant-institutional.svg`
  Best for a standards-framework feel. More restrained, balanced, and formal. This refined version adds a few inner-ring and near-boundary orbs to represent participation along a continuum rather than a rigid inside/outside divide.

- `halo-ring-variant-community.svg`
  Best for emphasizing participation, contribution, and visible community presence around the core.

- `halo-ring-variant-minimal-avatar.svg`
  Best for tiny-size legibility in avatar and favicon-like contexts.

- `halo-ring-variant-institutional-randomized.svg`
  A refinement of the institutional direction with a more irregular orb distribution, reducing the sense of visual weighting on one side while preserving the continuum symbolism.

## Shared Symbolism

- **Center** — core values, principles, and human-led intent
- **Rings** — framework layers, governance, order, and continuity
- **Orbs** — community participation, proposals, contributions, and evolving discussion
- **Inner-ring orbs** — people and organizations operating closer to HALOS's core principles and practices
- **Near-boundary orbs** — emerging ideas and participants exploring concepts that may later be integrated more fully
- **Gradients** — multiple perspectives brought into principled alignment
