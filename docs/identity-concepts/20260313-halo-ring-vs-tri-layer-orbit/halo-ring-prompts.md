# HALOS Concept Prompts: Halo Ring

## Direction

The Halo Ring direction treats HALOS as a structured framework with layered boundaries, governance, provenance, and durable clarity. The mark should feel calm, precise, and standards-oriented.

## Prompt 1

```text
Minimal logo for HALOS (Human-Agent Living Operating System), dark midnight navy background, a stable luminous center surrounded by segmented concentric rings in cool cyan and ice-blue, precise geometric spacing, subtle glow, institutional and framework-oriented rather than product-like, elegant and durable, no text, no photorealism, no generic circuit patterns, designed for a standards framework and open governance context.
```

## Prompt 2

```text
Premium symbolic mark for an open framework called HALOS, deep blue-black background, a calm central point with two or three segmented halo rings around it, refined geometry, restrained cyan-blue illumination, subtle silver-blue accents, clean negative space, modern but non-hype aesthetic, should suggest governance, provenance, and principled structure, no words, no mascot, no startup style.
```

## Prompt 3

```text
Abstract framework identity mark for HALOS, square format, dark navy background, concentric luminous halo segments arranged around a stable center, balanced radial composition, cool blue and pale ice highlights, precise architectural feeling, transparent and trustworthy tone, not sci-fi, not crypto, not a product badge, suitable for repository branding and documentation headers.
```

## Notes

- The center should feel stable and intentional.
- Ring segmentation should imply layered structure, not fragmentation.
- Glow should be restrained; clarity matters more than spectacle.
