# From dashboards to decisions

Dashboards gave us visibility.

But they don’t help us decide.

A human still has to:

- interpret the data  
- connect context  
- imagine actions  
- make a call  

We built:

- systems of record  
- systems of insight  

But not:

systems of reasoning

---

The future isn’t more dashboards.

It’s systems that:

- understand context  
- simulate outcomes  
- propose actions  
- learn from results
