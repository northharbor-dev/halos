# What is a Compounding Intelligence Organization?

A simple idea:

Every decision should make the next one better.

---

A system that:

- understands the business  
- reasons about it  
- acts on it  
- learns over time  

---

Core layers:

- operational systems  
- append-only ledger  
- storage + analytics  
- semantic graph  
- agents + workflows  
- decision loop  
- governance layer  

---

Over time, the organization doesn’t just operate.

It gets smarter.
