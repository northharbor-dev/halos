# Most companies don’t learn. They just repeat.

We like to think organizations get smarter over time.

But most don’t.

They collect data.  
They build dashboards.  
They hold meetings.  
They make decisions.  

And then they move on.

And then… it’s gone.

A week later, a month later, a year later—someone asks:

“Why did we decide that?”

Not in theory.  
In practice.

The context is gone.  
The reasoning is fuzzy.  
The alternatives are missing.  

The organization didn’t learn.

It just moved forward.

---

## The gap no one talks about

We’ve spent decades building:

- systems of record  
- systems of insight  

But we haven’t built:

systems that remember how decisions were made

---

Most companies don’t compound intelligence.

The next generation will.
