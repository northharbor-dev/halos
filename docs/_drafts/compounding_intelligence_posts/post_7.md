# Most AI investments won’t compound

AI is everywhere right now.

But most implementations look like this:

- copilots  
- assistants  
- isolated tools  

---

They improve tasks.

But they don’t improve the system.

---

The missing piece:

learning across decisions

---

Without:

- shared context  
- decision memory  
- feedback loops  

AI remains:

localized intelligence

---

The real opportunity:

systems where intelligence compounds

---

That’s the difference between:

incremental gains  
and exponential advantage
